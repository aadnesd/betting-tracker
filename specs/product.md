# Product Spec — Matched Betting Tracker

## Goal
Build a single‑user matched‑betting tracker that makes it effortless to plan, log, reconcile, and analyze matched bets across bookmakers and exchanges.

## Outcomes
- Clarity: obvious bet status and required action.
- Accuracy: precise stake, odds, liability, commission, and FX.
- Speed: quick entry, smart defaults, and AI‑assisted intake.
- Trust: transparent calculations and audit trail.
- Insight: profitability by bookmaker, promo, and time period.

## In Scope
- Matched sets (back + lay) with promo metadata.
- AI screenshot intake: upload → parse → review → save.
- Reconciliation workflow.
- Reporting and exposure tracking.
- CSV import/export.

## Out of Scope (for now)
- Multi‑user auth and team features.
- Live odds feeds.
- Automated placement with bookmakers/exchanges.
